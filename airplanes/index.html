N6827W

Price: $125 Hour, wet.

N6827W is a 1965 Piper Cherokee 140 with newer paint and an upgraded instrument panel. It is a four seat airplane with four place intercom, though full sized adults will have difficulty with the limited leg room in the back seats. It is equipped and certified for IFR with dual nav/com's featuring the Garmin GNS 430 GPS and S-Tec 20 autopilot. It is available for instruction and rent.
